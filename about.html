About the CareerConnect Portal

The CareerConnect Portal was developed as part of the Royal Nefsea International School Career Day initiative. It aims to capture student interests, parent insights, and booth data to build a meaningful bridge between education and the real world.

What Career Day Covers

Our Career Day is designed to inspire students to explore diverse career paths through interactive sessions, engaging presentations, and personal connections with professionals. It helps students:

  • Understand different fields such as Engineering, Technology, Medicine, and the Arts.
  • Develop confidence in pursuing their passions.
  • Engage directly with industry experts and mentors.
  • Learn the value of continuous education and skill development.
